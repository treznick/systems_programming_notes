    Fine Custom Made Leaf Dagger Damascus Blade and Black Walnut Scabbard and Handle

    From MScollectables

     
    This fine hand made leaf blade dagger has a Damascus steel blade od 15N20 and high Carbon 1095 steel, triple heat treated. The 9 3/8 inch blade is 1 1/2 inches wide at the widest part and 3/16 inches thick. The guard is layered brass and sterling silver with a Stirling silver decorative ring. The Handle is black walnut and the pommel is maple burl. The scabbard is black walnut with sterling cap. The over all length is 13 1/2 inches. This knife is made by me in the USA. Great collectable or gift.
